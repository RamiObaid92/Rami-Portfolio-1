About me

Hi, I'm Rami Obaid, a curious and enthusiastic learner based in Stockholm, Sweden. My journey into software development started with a simple yet persistent question: "How does it all work behind the scenes?" Spending most of my life around computers, I couldn't resist diving into the world of technology to find out.

Right now, I'm focused on mastering .NET and backend development, honing my skills to build efficient and scalable solutions. My goal is to contribute to meaningful projects that solve real-world problems while continuously growing as a developer.

When I'm not coding or exploring new technologies, you'll likely find me enjoying a good game as an avid gamer or recharging in the beauty of nature. Whether it's through code, gaming, or exploring the great outdoors, I'm always looking for the next adventure!
